Welcome to GlycoMe

Understand and personalize your postprandial glycemic response

What is PPGI?

PPGI is a personalized estimate of your glycemic response to a given food, computed as 100 ร IAUC(food) รท IAUC(glucose reference). The glucose reference is a 100 g portion with 16.7 g carbohydrate and 0 g protein/fat/fiber.

How GlycoMe works

  1. Collects your basic info (age, waist, activity, blood group)
  2. Uses food nutrient composition
  3. Predicts IAUC with a trained LightGBM model
  4. Converts IAUC to PPGI by normalizing to glucose reference
